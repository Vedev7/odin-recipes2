Crumble Recipe

Description

It's like a dessert breakfast! Dessert or breakfast. Dessert AND breakfast. All of the above! Personally, I enjoy it chilled.

However, you can heat up the apple and honey before adding the granola and yogurt if you'd prefer a hot healthy apple crumble parfait!

Perfect first thing in the morning OR as an evening post-dinner dessert/snack. SO delicious, y'all!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Fall-tastic Spice Granola
  • 1 Cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Apple
  • 2 tsps Honey

Steps

  1. Layer the granola, yogurt, apple slices, and honey for each parfait
  2. Serve and enjoy!
